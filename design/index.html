About Amanda's Mobile Spa

Amanda Holtzinger graduated massage school in 2013 and esthetics school in 2014.

Amanda has worked for several salons and spas in the area including the upscale Rough Creek Lodge and Resort in Glen Rose, TX. However, her passion remains in her private practice and the clients who enjoy her services.

Her ultimate goal is taking care of her clients. She supports her clients in their unique healing journey to whole health in a relaxing atmosphere, whether it be your house or Rock Paper Scissors Salon and Spa.

Since her graduation, Amanda has continued her education and training by studying the following areas:

  • Deep Tissue Massage Techniques
  • Pre-Natal, Post & Partum, Infant Massage Techniques
  • Correcting Shoulder Dysfunction Techniques
  • Sports Massage Techniques
  • Pelvic Stabilization Techniques
  • Hot Stone Techniques
  • Reiki Levels I & II
